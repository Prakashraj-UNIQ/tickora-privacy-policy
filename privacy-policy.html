Privacy Policy

At Tickora, your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using our mobile or web apps.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address
  • Work hours and attendance logs
  • Device and usage information (e.g., IP address, OS, browser)
  • Location data (if permission is granted)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and track attendance
  • To generate reports and timesheets
  • To enhance user experience and app functionality
  • To maintain system security and prevent misuse

3. Permissions

Tickora may request access to sensitive features such as your device's location. All permissions are explained via just-in-time prompts within the app before any data is accessed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Some third-party services (e.g., analytics, cloud storage) may have access under strict contractual obligations to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for providing our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, updates, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. We respond to all privacy-related requests in a timely manner.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: tickoratech@gmail.com
